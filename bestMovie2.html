Why I like the movie

This is my favorite movie because I have very fond memories of watching the original series. When I found that there was a remake, I was very excited to see it. Out of the remakes so far, this one is my favorite because it has more action than the first movie, and is more faithful to the originals and is less confusing than the third. The movie has excellent action and has better animation than the original series, but the movie also focuses a lot on character development and how they interact with each other. The characters relatable and the movie makes you care about them. The soundtrack is also great; the ochestra and choir vocals mesh well with the action on screen.

Favorite Actor

Just because this movie is my favorite doesn't mean that I have a favorite actor within the movie. All the voice actors did an exceptional job portraying their characters and emotions. If there were a character within the movie that I relate the most with, it would be Shinji. He is doing things to for others and rarely does things for himself. I think that I focus too much on what others think about me and am constantly doing things to please others and forge better social relationships.
